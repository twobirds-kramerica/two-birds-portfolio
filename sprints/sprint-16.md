## Opening Scenario

Frank, 74, wanted to sell his late wife's sewing machine. His neighbour told him to list it "online." Frank found a marketplace app, took a photo, and posted it for $50. Within an hour he had three messages. The first asked him to accept payment by e-transfer and ship the machine to another province. The second offered $200 if Frank would click a link to "verify his account." The third was a real person from his town who wanted to come see it. Frank couldn't tell which was safe.

Start from this moment. Then teach.

## Sections to Build

1. **Frank's Three Messages** — break down each message. Red flags in #1 (overpayment + shipping scam), red flags in #2 (phishing link), green flags in #3 (local, wants to see in person).
2. **How Online Marketplaces Work** — plain language. You post, people respond, you meet or ship. Free to list. The platform takes a cut or doesn't. The platform does NOT verify buyers.
3. **Buying Safely** — meet in public, bring someone, pay cash or e-transfer on the spot. Never click payment links. Never pay before seeing the item. If the deal seems too good, it is.
4. **Selling Safely** — take clear photos, write honest descriptions, set a fair price (search for similar items first). Accept cash or e-transfer only. Meet in a public place. Never give out your home address in the listing.
5. **The Scam Spotting Checklist** — visual checklist card. Red flags: asks to move off-platform, offers more than asking price, sends a link, requests personal info, won't meet in person.
6. **E-Transfer Safety** — how Interac e-Transfer works for marketplace sales. Set a security question. Verify payment received before handing over the item. Never accept a screenshot as proof.
7. **Shipping: When and How** — only ship if you use the platform's built-in shipping (which provides tracking and protection). Never ship and trust the buyer to pay later.
8. **What to Do If Something Goes Wrong** — report to the platform, report to local police (Canadian Anti-Fraud Centre: 1-888-495-8501), block the person, do not engage further.
9. **Quiz** — 5 questions
10. **What You Learned** — summary bullets
