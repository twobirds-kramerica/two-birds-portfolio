# Business Development Review Framework — For Mike K's Network

**Prepared for:** Aaron Patzalek → Mike Kerkvliet conversation
**Date:** April 4, 2026
**Purpose:** Offer a structured AI readiness review as value-add to Mike's economic development work.

---

## The Offer to Mike

> "I'll review any business you refer to me, free of charge, and give you a written summary you can share with them. If they want to go deeper, that becomes a paid engagement. But the initial review — that's a gift to your network."

---

## What the Review Covers

A structured 60-minute conversation covering:

### Part 1 — Business Context (15 min)
- What do you do? Who are your customers?
- How many people? What's your biggest operational pain?
- What technology do you use today? (Most answer: "QuickBooks and Gmail")

### Part 2 — AI Readiness Assessment (20 min)
- Where does manual data entry happen?
- What processes repeat identically every week?
- Where do mistakes cost you money?
- What information do you wish you had faster?
- Have you tried any AI tools? (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.)

### Part 3 — Quick Win Identification (15 min)
- Based on what I've heard, here are 3 things AI could help with in your business
- For each: what tool, what it does, estimated time savings, estimated cost
- No jargon — describe what changes in their daily life

### Part 4 — Recommendation (10 min)
- "Based on this conversation, I'd recommend [one of three paths]:"
  - **Path A:** You're good — use the quick wins I've identified, no further help needed
  - **Path B:** A full AI Workflow Audit (CA$2,500) would surface deeper opportunities
  - **Path C:** You need ongoing fractional AI support (CA$4,000/month retainer)

## The Written Output

After each review, Aaron delivers a 1-page summary:

**To the business owner:**
- 3 quick wins with tools and estimated savings
- Recommended next step (Path A, B, or C)
- Link to Clarity diagnostic for future reference

**To Mike (with owner's permission):**
- Business name and industry
- AI readiness level (None / Experimenting / Implementing)
- Top opportunity identified
- Whether they're a candidate for deeper engagement
