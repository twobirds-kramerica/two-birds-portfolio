## Conversion Notes

- **Email 1** is the most important — speed of response signals professionalism
- **Email 2** offers an easy "yes" (reply "send it") that keeps the conversation alive
- **Email 3** gives value regardless of outcome, builds goodwill for referrals
- **Never send Email 4** — three touches is enough. If they're not interested, respect it.
- **If they book the call:** Use the 30 minutes to listen first, present second. End with: "Based on what I've heard, I think the AI Workflow Audit would surface [X, Y, Z] for you. Would you like me to send a formal proposal?"
- **If they say no:** Thank them, ask if you can check back in 90 days. Add to CRM.
