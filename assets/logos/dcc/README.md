# Digital Confidence Centre — Logo

## Brand Context

The Digital Confidence Centre (DCC) is a free digital literacy programme for Canadian seniors, built by Two Birds Innovation. It helps people who feel overwhelmed by technology learn at their own pace, with no registration required.

**Mission:** "You are not behind. You are right on time."

**Audience:** Primarily seniors 65+ in Ontario, but welcoming to anyone feeling overwhelmed by technology.

**Tone:** Warm, patient, encouraging, safe. "Kitchen table with a cup of tea" feeling.

## Relationship to Two Birds Innovation

DCC is a **child brand** of Two Birds Innovation. It has its own visual identity, distinct from the parent company:

| Aspect | Two Birds Innovation | Digital Confidence Centre |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------------|
| **Logo** | Chevrons (code brackets) | TBD — Aaron selects from 8 variations |
| **Colour** | Blue (#0066CC) | Warm Teal (#00897B) |
| **Tone** | Direct, challenging, professional | Warm, patient, encouraging |
| **Audience** | B2B (library directors, credit unions) | B2C (seniors, families) |
| **Feel** | Tech company | Community centre |

Someone seeing both logos should NOT immediately think "same company." DCC should feel like its own welcoming, warm thing.
