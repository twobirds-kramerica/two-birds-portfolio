## Design Specification (v1.1)

### Canvas
- Dimensions: 1024 x 1024 px
- Background: #0066CC (solid blue)
- Corner radius: 180 px (app icon / favicon feel)
- Texture overlay: subtle cosmos effect — ~60 small circles (2-6 px radius), white at 8-15% opacity, scattered across background. Clipped to rounded rect.

### Top Chevron (east-pointing ">")
- Colour: #FFFFFF (white)
- Stroke-based, stroke-width 48 px
- Rounded line caps and joins
- Circle #1 (tip): solid white, radius 28 px, at east tip (pointed end)
- Circle #2 (open end): solid white, radius 28 px, at upper arm endpoint (top-left of open mouth)
- Position: upper portion, slightly left of centre

### Bottom Chevron (west-pointing "<")
- Colour: #FFFFFF (white)
- Stroke-based, stroke-width 48 px
- Rounded line caps and joins
- Circle #1 (tip): solid white, radius 28 px, at west tip (pointed end)
- Circle #2 (open end): solid white, radius 28 px, at lower arm endpoint (bottom-right of open mouth)
- Position: lower portion, slightly right of centre

### Composition
- Entire chevron composition centred within canvas
- Chevrons offset vertically and horizontally
- Paths would intersect if extended — converging trajectories
- Minimum padding: 140 px from canvas edges on all sides
- Composition bounding box is symmetrically centred at (512, 512)

---

## Design Intent

- **Two birds** = Aaron's twin daughters (fraternal, not identical)
- **Chevron shape** = coding brackets, forward motion, technology tie-in
- **Opposite directions** = different perspectives on a shared path
- **Both white** = unified brand, cleaner read, print-friendly
- **Cosmos texture** = interconnectedness, family's place in the universe
- **Open-end circles (asymmetric)** = fraternal twin daughters
  - Top chevron upper-left dot = one daughter
  - Bottom chevron lower-right dot = the other daughter
- **Tip circles** = anchor points, consistency from v1.0

## Usage Guidelines

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 32 px (below this the chevrons lose legibility)
- Print: 12 mm

### Clear Space
- Maintain at least 25% of the logo width as clear space on all sides
- No text, other logos, or busy backgrounds within the clear zone

### Do
- Use the SVG for any scalable context
- Use on white, dark, or blue backgrounds (the rounded blue background provides its own frame)
- Reference the exact hex values above for brand consistency

### Don't
- Stretch or distort the aspect ratio
- Change the chevron colour (white only in v1.1)
- Remove or reposition any of the four identity dots
- Add drop shadows, gradients, or outlines
- Place on a background that clashes with #0066CC
- Rotate the logo
