## Opening Scenario

Dorothy, 80, moved to a retirement home in London, Ontario after her husband passed. Her three children live in Toronto, Calgary, and Halifax. They set up a "family group chat" but Dorothy has never opened the app. She's afraid of pressing the wrong button. She misses hearing their voices but doesn't want to "bother" them with phone calls. Her granddaughter in Toronto video-calls every Sunday but Dorothy often misses it because she doesn't hear the notification. She feels more isolated than she has ever been — not because the tools don't exist, but because nobody taught her how to use them.

Start from this moment. Then teach.

## Sections to Build

1. **Dorothy's Missed Calls** — the emotional cost of digital isolation. This is not about technology — it is about connection. Frame the module as giving Dorothy her family back.
2. **The Three Ways to Stay in Touch** — voice calls (familiar), text/messaging (easy once set up), video calls (the closest to being there). Visual: warmth scale from least to most personal.
3. **Text Messaging Basics** — how to send a text, how to send a photo, how to use voice-to-text (dictation) so you never have to type. Step-by-step with iPad screenshots described.
4. **Group Chats Explained** — what a group chat is, how to read it, how to reply, how to mute notifications without leaving the group. The etiquette: you don't have to reply to everything.
5. **Video Calling Step by Step** — FaceTime (already on iPad), how to make a call, how to answer, how to adjust volume, how to flip camera, how to end call. Include: it's okay to just wave and say "I love you" — calls don't need to be long.
6. **Making Notifications Work for You** — how to turn up the volume, set a distinctive ringtone for family, enable notification sounds for messaging apps. So Dorothy never misses a Sunday call again.
7. **Voice Messages** — how to send and receive voice messages in messaging apps. For seniors who find typing difficult, this is the best feature. Hold the button, talk, release. Done.
8. **Staying Safe** — don't answer video calls from unknown numbers. Don't accept messages from strangers. How to block someone. How to report spam.
9. **Quiz** — 5 questions
10. **What You Learned** — summary bullets
