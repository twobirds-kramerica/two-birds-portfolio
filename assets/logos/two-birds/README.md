# Two Birds Innovation Logo

**Version:** 1.0
**Date created:** April 9, 2026
**Designer:** Aaron Patzalek / Two Birds Innovation

---

## Design Specification

### Canvas
- Dimensions: 1024 x 1024 px
- Background: #0066CC (solid blue)
- Corner radius: 180 px (app icon / favicon feel)

### Top Chevron (east-pointing ">")
- Colour: #E91E63 (fuchsia)
- Stroke-based, stroke-width 48 px
- Rounded line caps and joins
- Identity dot: solid circle, radius 28 px, at east tip
- Position: upper half, slightly left of centre

### Bottom Chevron (west-pointing "<")
- Colour: #FF88D8 (light pink)
- Stroke-based, stroke-width 48 px
- Rounded line caps and joins
- Identity dot: solid circle, radius 28 px, at west tip
- Position: lower half, slightly right of centre

### Composition
- Chevrons offset vertically and horizontally
- Paths would intersect if extended — converging trajectories
- Minimum padding: 120 px from canvas edges

---

## Design Intent

- **Two birds** = Aaron's twin daughters (fraternal, not identical)
- **Chevron shape** = coding brackets, forward motion, technology tie-in
- **Opposite directions** = different perspectives on a shared path
- **Colour variation** (fuchsia vs light pink) = fraternal — related but distinct
- **Single dot per chevron** = individual identity marker, one per child

## Usage Guidelines

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 32 px (below this the chevrons lose legibility)
- Print: 12 mm

### Clear Space
- Maintain at least 25% of the logo width as clear space on all sides
- No text, other logos, or busy backgrounds within the clear zone

### Do
- Use the SVG for any scalable context
- Use on white, dark, or blue backgrounds (the rounded blue background provides its own frame)
- Reference the exact hex values above for brand consistency

### Don't
- Stretch or distort the aspect ratio
- Change the chevron colours
- Remove or reposition the identity dots
- Add drop shadows, gradients, or outlines
- Place on a background that clashes with #0066CC
- Rotate the logo
