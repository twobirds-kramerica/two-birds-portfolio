## Panel Reviews

### Theo (Brand Strategist) — APPROVED with notes
The site uses Two Birds Blue correctly via tokens.css. Typography is Inter. The hero headline is strong — "20 years building products for millions" positions experience before the founder identity. The career timeline and skills grid reinforce credibility. **Note:** The "What People Say" section is empty — it signals incompleteness. Either populate it or remove it before launch. The headshot placeholder (blue circle with "AP") is acceptable for beta but not for production.

### Maya (Content Director) — APPROVED with notes
Copy is clean, direct, and free of banned words. Canadian English throughout. The DCC featured project description is excellent — "anxiety-first design" and "Brenda" persona give it texture that generic portfolio copy lacks. **Note:** The 1-page bio in positioning.md is stronger than what's on the site — consider pulling more of that language into the About section, especially the "arrive, map the system, strip the waste" line. The "Coming soon" badges on Clarity and Career Coach are honest but risk making the portfolio feel thin — consider adding more detail about what these will do.

### Priya (QA / Testing) — APPROVED
Skip link present. All form inputs have linked labels. ARIA labels on nav, decorative elements marked `aria-hidden="true"`. Heading hierarchy: h1 → h2 → h3 → h4, no skips. Mobile hamburger has `aria-expanded`. CSP meta tag present. JSON-LD Person schema present. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected. **Note:** No `og:image` tag — will need a social sharing image before production launch.

### Kai (Social / Community) — REWORK (minor)
No `og:image` meta tag. When someone shares this on LinkedIn, it will show no preview image. This is critical for a professional portfolio. **Fix:** Add an `og:image` — either a screenshot of the site, the Two Birds logo, or a branded social card. Also: the LinkedIn link in the CTA section should be more prominent — it's the primary professional network for Aaron's audience.

### Drew (Program Director) — APPROVED with conditions
Site meets Stage 3 DoD baseline. Code committed, no placeholder content in user-visible areas (except testimonials section and headshot, both documented). Canadian English, no secrets, CSP present, JSON-LD present. **Conditions for production (Stage 4):** (1) Add og:image, (2) populate or remove testimonials section, (3) replace headshot placeholder, (4) verify Formspree endpoint is correct and tested.
