## Role Type 1: Safeguards / Trust & Safety Analyst

**Example titles from real postings (Anthropic, Apr 2026):**
- Safeguards Analyst, Account Abuse
- Safeguards Enforcement Analyst, Safety Evaluations
- Safeguards Policy Analyst, Fraud & Scams
- Enforcement Operations Lead

**Companies that hire for this:**
- Anthropic (active postings, some remote US)
- OpenAI (Trust & Safety team)
- Google DeepMind (AI Safety)
- Meta (Integrity team)

**Typical requirements:**
- Experience in abuse detection, policy analysis, or trust/safety operations
- Analytical thinking and attention to detail
- Familiarity with AI systems (not ML engineering)
- No CS degree required at Anthropic ("About half our technical staff had no prior ML experience")

**Salary range (CAD, estimated):**
- CA$80,000-130,000 for analyst level
- CA$130,000-180,000 for lead/manager level

**Remote-friendly:** Some roles are remote (US). Canadian remote unclear. Several list "Remote, SF, DC, NYC."
**Timeline to land:** 2-4 months (application + interview process)
**Aaron's match:** 55%. Strong analytical and product evaluation background. Gap: no direct trust/safety experience. The "fraud & scams" angle connects to DCC (scam modules for seniors).

**Key finding from Anthropic's careers page:** "We care about what you can do, not where you learned to do it. About half our technical staff had no prior ML experience." This is the most encouraging signal for Aaron's profile.

**Honest flag:** These are US-market salaries in many cases. Canadian-based remote roles may pay 15-25% less. Canadian AI companies (Cohere, Element AI successors) may offer closer to the lower end of these ranges.

**Canadian-specific opportunity:** Cohere is headquartered in Toronto. They are the most likely AI company to hire Canadians in Canadian time zones at Canadian market rates. Their job board was inaccessible during research (404 error) and should be checked directly.
