# Master Brand Ideation & Vetting Ledger

Project Focus: Innovation Lab & Consultancy (AI, Digital Literacy, SME Transformation)
Founder: Aaron Patzalek

## 1. The Core Philosophy & Brand Foundation
- The Mission: To empower people through high-perspective innovation and supportive enablement.
- Engagement Model ("Lead from the Back"): The consultancy provides the engine and the vantage point; the user sits at the steering wheel and drives their own progress.
- The Framework: Essentialism (stripping away 60% of operational noise) + Lovability (designing for deep human resonance).
- The Motto: Always forward. Never quit. Grow bravely. Support with care.
- The Mantra: Be YOUR best self, not someone else's.

## 2. The AI Paradigm: Sovereignty over Autonomy
- Autonomy (What competitors do): Building tools that act for the user, ultimately replacing human effort.
- Sovereignty (Our brand): Building tools that empower the user to act for themselves. The user retains ownership of their data, their logic, and their destiny.

## 3. The Visual Anchor & Origin Story
The visual identity is anchored by the >< symbol (offset chevrons).
- The Fraternal Twins Origin: Inspired by twin daughters. Represents that ideas, growth, and people can start at the exact same point but must be empowered to travel in uniquely different, sovereign directions.
- Navigational Forces: The intersection of headwinds (the challenge of change/displacement) and tailwinds (the momentum of AI).
- The Intersection: The exact point where the "Honest Helper" (the consultancy) meets the "Brave User."

## 4. The Branding Graveyard (Blacklist — never revisit)
- ALTO / ALTO INNOVATION — collides with Silicon Valley tech agency and Transport Canada train project
- TBI / TBI INNOVATION — Traumatic Brain Injury acronym
- DUOALTO / DUALTO — J&J surgical device trademark
- AUTOS — automotive industry confusion
- AXIA — too clinical, lacks humanity
- VITA / VITE — means "Hurry" in French
- KORE — means "Nothing/Void" in Maori

## 5. Active Backlog (candidates to revisit)
