# Test Personas — Two Birds Innovation

**Date:** April 5, 2026
**Purpose:** Every product decision is validated against these personas.

---

## Persona 1 — Brenda (DCC Primary User)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 74 |
| Device | iPad (iOS), given by daughter for Christmas |
| Location | St. Thomas, Ontario |
| Tech comfort | Low — afraid of pressing wrong button |
| Primary concern | Safety and trust |
| Internet | 25 Mbps home Wi-Fi, doesn't know her password |
| Vision | Reads at 150% zoom, slight macular degeneration |
| Language | English primary, some French |

### Test Scenarios
- [ ] Can she find the module she needs in under 30 seconds?
- [ ] Can she read all text without zooming beyond 150%?
- [ ] Can she tap all buttons without mis-tapping adjacent elements?
- [ ] Does she feel reassured by the tone (not patronised)?
- [ ] Can she switch to French and back without losing her place?
- [ ] Does the confidence check every 500 words feel supportive?
- [ ] Can she print a cheat sheet?

## Persona 4 — Library Director (DCC B2B Buyer)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 55 |
| Device | Desktop Windows (corporate) |
| Location | Ontario (any municipality) |
| Tech comfort | Medium-high |
| Primary concern | Credibility, compliance, board approval |

### Test Scenarios
- [ ] Does the site look like a CA$15k/year product?
- [ ] Is WCAG compliance evidenced? (badge, statement, or test results)
- [ ] Is bilingual capability immediately obvious?
- [ ] Is the pilot offer clear and low-risk enough to approve without board vote?
- [ ] Can they find the pitch deck and share it with their team?
- [ ] Is there a way to see it running before committing?
