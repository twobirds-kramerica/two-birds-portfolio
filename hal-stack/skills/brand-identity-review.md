# Brand Identity Review

**Domain:** Branding
**Layer Compatibility:** L1-L4

## What It Does
Evaluates a logo or visual brand asset for strength, balance, scalability, print/screen consistency, and storytelling alignment with the Two Birds Innovation brand.

## Prerequisites
- Access to the brand spec (`assets/logos/two-birds/README.md`)
- The asset to review (SVG or PNG)

## Instructions

1. **Verify brand colours.** Check every colour against the brand spec hex values. Flag any deviations.
2. **Test at multiple sizes.** View at 1024px, 512px, 256px, 32px (favicon), and 16px. Does the design remain legible at every size?
3. **Check print readiness.** Are there gradients, transparency, or cosmos textures that won't reproduce in CMYK? Flag them.
4. **Assess visual balance.** Is the composition centred? Does it feel top-heavy, bottom-heavy, or cramped? Is negative space used well?
5. **Evaluate strength.** Does the logo command attention, or does it feel delicate/timid? Would you notice it at the top of a slide deck?
6. **Test the story.** Does the fraternal-not-identical theme come through? Can you see "two birds, different paths, connected"?
7. **Check stroke consistency.** Are line weights consistent (or intentionally varied for fraternal effect)? Are corners and caps rounded?
8. **Verify identity markers.** Are circles at chevron tips present and correctly sized?

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] All colours match brand spec
- [ ] Legible at 16px
- [ ] No print-hostile effects (gradients, transparency)
- [ ] Visually balanced and centred
- [ ] Commands strength — not delicate
- [ ] Fraternal difference visible
- [ ] Stroke consistency verified
