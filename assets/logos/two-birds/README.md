## Official Logo: V05

V05 was selected as the official Two Birds Innovation logo. It features white chevrons on a blue (#0066CC) background with subtle cosmos texture (semi-transparent white dots). Identity circles are larger than other variations (34px radius) for better visibility at small sizes. Fraternal differentiation via asymmetric open-end circle placement: upper arm on top chevron, lower arm on bottom chevron.

## Design Specification

- **Background:** #0066CC (solid blue) with cosmos texture (~30 semi-transparent white dots)
- **Corner radius:** 180px
- **Chevrons:** White (#FFFFFF), stroke-width 52px, rounded caps and joins
- **Identity circles:** 34px radius, white, at all four endpoints (tips + asymmetric open ends)
- **Canvas:** 1024×1024px
- **Minimum padding:** ~185px from edges
